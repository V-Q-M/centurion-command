# Roman battle game!

This game simulates fighting a roman battle as a centurion.

## Current idea

You play as a centurion and command the frontline.

You can:
- Change formation 
    - Battle formation: Offensive bonus, melee defense bonus, movement penalty
    - Testudo formation: Offensive penalty, range defense bonus, huge movement penalty
- Push forward: Advances forward if enemy flees
- Swap frontline: Switches out soldiers at the front, which refreshes stamina and strength of the frontline
- Retreat: Fall back safely

You win when the enemy surrenders or routes
You lose when you surrender or route.
